5. Griffith Observatory

A place where the stars can see the stars, it's located in Griffith Park. There's also a planetarium.

6. Grauman's Chinese Theatre

Located on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, the famous movie theatre was named after Sid Grauman, an entrepreneur who had opened up a chain of movie theatres in other states before moving to Hollywood in the early 1900s. Now it's the sight of the famous cementing of stars hands, feet, and sugnatures, movie premieres and the Oscar Awards in the 1940s. And it's a popular movie theatre for LA tourists.
