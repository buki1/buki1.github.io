Cool Sights To See in Los Angeles

1. Santa Monica Pier

Home of the famous ferris wheel (known as the Pacific Wheel) and Pacific Park amusement park situated next to the beach. It's similar to Brooklyn's Coney Island Amusement Park.

2. Universal Studios Hollywood

A go-to attraction for visitors to LA. Known for rides like "The Wizarding World of Harry Potter", "The Simpsons Ride" and its world famous studio tour. Universal Citywalk is also located there.

3. Disneyland

Consisting of two parks: Disneyland Park and Disney California Adventure. Opened in 1955, there are eight themed lands: Main Street USA, Tomorrowland, Fantasyland, Mickey's Toontown, Frontierland, Critter County, New Orleans Square, and Adventureland. Popular rides include Pirates of the Carribean, Space Mountain, and Splash Mountain.

4. Hollywood Walk of Fame

Marilyn Monroe, Michael Jackson, and Donald Trump all have stars on the iconic stretch of fifteen blocks along Hollywood Boulevard. More stars are nominated for a star and added everyday.
